RECOMMENDATIONS

This research enlightens the integration of financial metrics into neural network models for ESG ratings prediction. These empirical-based recommendations are to guide practitioners toward enhancing the applicability and reliability of ESG ratings.

First of all

Adoption of Standardized Reporting

Advocacy for a standardized ESG reporting framework

This harmonization would reconcile the present contradictions among ESG rating providers by harmonizing metrics and benchmarks used, hence making them comparable and raising ESG evaluation reliability for investment. 

Transparency and Accessibility of ESG

It is encouraged to shift from voluntary to mandatory policies for comprehensive ESG disclosures. 

Integration of ESG into Operational and Risk Management

Such integration not only aligns companies with sustainable practices but also potentially enhances their long-term profitability and risk profile.  

However, ESG strategies should be tailored to reflect specific industry and geography characteristics, especially for international business. 

Enhance Revenue Transparency and Disclosure

Implementation is needed through regular updating and publication of detailed financial reports that would reflect sustainability investments and ensure the desired transparency in how revenue supports ESG and proactive communications of financial health and sustainability projects. 

Leverage Neural Network Model for ESG Predictions

ESG predictor tool

Investors and stakeholders can get access to this model for more standardized ESG evaluations globally, facilitating better-informed investment decisions and implementing sustainability practices.
